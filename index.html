About Me

I'm a Senior Full-Stack Engineer with a background in Human-Computer Interaction, currently working on complex laboratory software systems at Boehringer Ingelheim. My work bridges the gap between laboratory requirements, engineering excellence, and production-grade reliability.

I own the entire application lifecycle: from UX research and interface design through React/Node.js development to PostgreSQL data modeling and OpenShift/Jenkins cloud orchestration. I've delivered systems used daily by research scientists in regulated pharmaceutical environments.

Beyond software, I'm passionate about hands-on making — from 3D printing functional prototypes to woodworking furniture pieces. This maker mindset deeply influences how I approach software: with care for craft, usability, and lasting quality.
