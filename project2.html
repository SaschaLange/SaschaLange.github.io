0711 SXP - Spätzle Express Racing Team

Bicycle kit design with a local touch.

I designed a bicycle jersey to equip a team of five friends racing a 4-stage tour from Munich to Jesolo crossing the Alps. With the support of the local company Settele GmbH I was able to create a jersey that combined the traditonal german food "Spätzle" with modern typography and homages to urban culture in and around Stuttgart.

Project History

Cycling has become a big passion for me within the last year. I started track cycling and immersing myself into the bike culture of Hamburg. Not son thereafter I bought my first roadbike and from there on more and more time was spent riding longer and longer tours.

As winter 2017 came along and the weather prevented me from riding I was looking for new challenges to rise the ba for myself in the coming summer of 2018. I decided to join the RadRace "Tour de Friends 2018" to set a big goal for the end of summer and push myself during training sessions throughout the year. A couple of my friends were motivated by my enthusiasm and signed up shortly thereafter. I pushed the idea of a team cycling kit for group solidarity and uniform appearance. We all share the same region where we grew up in so it came naturally that we wanted to create a jersey that was good looking, subtle but rewards the eye if you look closer and see the connections.

0711 (Null Sieben Elf) is the area code of the municipal Stuttgart as well as a genereal reference to urban culture and Hip-Hop in the capital of Bade-Würtemberg.

With Settele GmbH - market leader for Spätzle - we found a sponsor that was onboard with the idea and kindly provided us with the funds to print jerseys for out team.

Insights

In general this was a quite easy project without many hickups or complications. The concept did slightly change in the course of the design process because a company kindly declined our request. Fortunately Baden-Würtemberg has many culinary dishes that could be incoperated into the overall theme of the jersey. Once again it was interesting to see how much the spirit of different people can be increased by focusing on a common goal and connect through such a simple thing like the uniform appearance of a team jersey.

I am very happy how the design work transferred from the screen to the real world and wish the best of luck to my team for the upcoming tour.
